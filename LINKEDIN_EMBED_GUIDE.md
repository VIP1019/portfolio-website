# LinkedIn Embed Posts - Quick Guide

## 🎯 How to Get Your LinkedIn Embed Code

### Step 1: Go to Your LinkedIn Post
1. Open your LinkedIn profile: https://www.linkedin.com/in/princejheck-juan-27145a3a8
2. Find the post you want to embed

### Step 2: Get Embed Code
1. Click the **"..."** (three dots) button on the post
2. Select **"Embed this post"**
3. Choose **"Embed full post"** (shows image and text)
4. Click **"Copy code"**

### Step 3: Send Me the Embed Codes
Copy the embed codes for all your posts and paste them here.

The code will look like:
```html
<iframe src="https://www.linkedin.com/embed/feed/update/urn:li:share:123456789" 
        height="600" 
        width="100%" 
        frameborder="0" 
        allowfullscreen="" 
        title="Embedded post">
</iframe>
```
