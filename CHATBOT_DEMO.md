# 🤖 AI Chatbot Demo - Quick Start Guide

## How to Access the Chatbot

1. **Open your Skills page**: Navigate to `skills.php` in your browser
2. **Look for the purple chat button**: You'll see it in the bottom-right corner with "Ask me anything!" badge
3. **Click to open**: The chat window will slide up
