🔧 Fix EmailJS 422 Error

The Most Common Issue & How to Fix It

❌ Error You're Seeing:

Failed to load resource: the server responded with a status of 422

What it means: EmailJS received your request but rejected it because something is misconfigured in your template.

⚠️ MOST LIKELY CAUSE:

The "To Email" field in your template settings is set to {{email}} instead of your actual email address!

✅ THE FIX (2 Minutes):

Step 1: Open Your Template

🔗 Open Template in EmailJS

Step 2: Look at the RIGHT SIDEBAR

You should see a "Settings" section with these fields:

  • To Email - This is the problem!
  • From Name
  • From Email
  • Reply To

Step 3: Check "To Email" Field

❌ WRONG:

{{email}}

This won't work because {{email}} is a variable that gets replaced, but EmailJS needs a fixed destination!


✅ CORRECT:

princejheckjuan023@gmail.com

This is YOUR email where you want to receive testimonial notifications!

Step 4: Other Settings to Check

From Name: Can leave as default or set to "Portfolio Testimonial"

From Email: Use default EmailJS email

Reply To: Set to {{from_email}} so you can reply directly to the person

Step 5: Save & Test

  1. Click the SAVE button (top right)
  2. Go back to your website
  3. Press F5 to refresh
  4. Submit a test testimonial
  5. Check your email! 📧

📸 What Your Template Settings Should Look Like:

Settings Tab (Right Sidebar):
┌─────────────────────────────────┐
│ Subject *                       │

│ To Email *                      │
